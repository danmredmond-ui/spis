The Problem

Hypergrowth sales organizations are adding hundreds of enterprise sellers to meet accelerating demand for AI-native security solutions. Every month a new seller spends learning rather than selling is a month of quota attainment deferred, and at this pace that cost compounds across an entire hiring class simultaneously. The industry average ramp for an enterprise seller in a complex security category is seven months. The best-in-class teams reach full productivity in three and a half. That gap is not a hiring quality problem. It is a systems problem. ARIA is the system.

ARIA provides a holistic view of onboarding and training — connecting assessment scores, activity milestones, and prospect engagements into a single ramp health score for every seller. The goal is not just to measure whether a rep completed a module, but whether they are booking meetings, building pipeline, and on pace to close their first deal within 90 days.

Step-by-Step — Live Demo Walkthrough

Follow these steps to explore the full ARIA experience in under 10 minutes.

1

Open the live demo

Open Chrome and navigate to the demo URL above. You will see the ARIA login screen with two buttons. Click Launch Demo Mode — No API Key Required. The full application opens immediately.

2

Explore the Manager Dashboard

You land on the Manager Dashboard in Manager View. You will see the $10.5M recoverable quota capacity headline, three rep health cards — Jamie in green, Alex in amber, and Morgan in red — and the AI Learning Gap Analysis panel at the bottom. Click Generate Analysis to run the AI cohort analysis. It returns in about two seconds with specific coaching priorities across the team.

3

Switch to Rep View

Click the Rep View button in the top right next to the Manager View toggle. Use the dropdown beside it to switch between Alex Rivera, Jamie Okafor, and Morgan Chen. Each rep shows a different ramp health state, progress across modules, and today's priority actions. Morgan Chen's profile (red card) is the most compelling to click through — strong assessment scores with zero pipeline activity at day 61.

4

Run a Training Module Assessment

Click Training Center in the top navigation. Select any module — Module 4 Competitive Positioning is the most relevant to explore. Read through the content sections, then click Continue to Assessment. Read the Microsoft Purview competitive scenario and write your response in the text box. Click Submit for AI Evaluation. Claude returns a scored evaluation with dimension-level written feedback in under 15 seconds.

5

Try the Presentation Lab

Click Presentation Lab in the top navigation. Read the Visa Inc. account brief and the five call objectives. Choose Option B — Written Submission and type how you would open a 15-minute CISO discovery call with Karen Liu. Aim for 200 words or more. Click Submit Written Assessment. Claude evaluates it across five dimensions and returns a full scoring report with coaching priorities.

6

Review the 30/60/90 Plan

Click 30/60/90 Plan in the top navigation. The three phase buttons at the top switch between Phase 1, Phase 2, and Phase 3. The current phase for the active rep is highlighted. Each action item is color-coded by type — blue for activity, purple for assessment, amber for presentation, red for milestone. Items verified automatically from Salesforce show the ⚡ Salesforce badge.
